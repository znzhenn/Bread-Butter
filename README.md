Bread & Butter is a cozy bakery management game where players bake bread, serve a variety of customers, and gradually grow a small bakery. The game focuses on relaxed bread-making, light management, and friendly customer interactions.

Players earn revenue and feedback from customers to unlock new recipes, upgrade kitchen equipment, and improve the bakery over time.



## Project Status
**Early development / prototype phase**

Core gameplay systems are being designed and implemented. Visuals and content are limited at the moment in order to keep scope manageable.


## Core Gameplay Loop

**Prepare ‚Üí Bake ‚Üí Serve ‚Üí Upgrade**
- Gather and manage ingredients  
- Bake bread using interactive mechanics  
- Serve customers with individual preferences  
- Upgrade the bakery and unlock new content  


## Key Features

- Cozy, low-pressure bakery management
- Interactive bread-baking process
- Multiple bread types with increasing complexity
- Customers with preferences and memory
- Progression through upgrades and recipes
- Persistent bakery state across days


## Gameplay Overview

### Baking Bread
Players bake bread through a series of steps:
- Learning a recipe
- Collecting ingredients
- Combining ingredients correctly
- Waiting for dough to rise (proofing)
- Decorating bread
- Baking with temperature and timing interactions

Each step affects internal bread attributes such as:
- Overall quality
- Texture
- Sale value
- Customer satisfaction


### Customers
- Customers arrive with individual preferences
- If desired bread is available, they purchase immediately
- Otherwise, they may request a specific bread to be baked
- Customers provide feedback through dialogue and ratings
- Satisfied customers may return and become friendlier over time



### Progression & Upgrades
- Unlock new recipes
- Upgrade kitchen equipment
- Hire kitchen helpers
- Improve bakery appearance
- Increase efficiency and customer satisfaction



## Bread Types
Recipes are unlocked through progression and include:
- White Loaf
- Whole Wheat
- Baguette
- Sourdough
- Sweet Bread
- Flatbread

Each recipe has:
- Difficulty level
- Preparation time
- Base value
- Customer preference modifiers
